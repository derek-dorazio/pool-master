# Actors

## Root Admin

Goal:
- keep the platform healthy with as little routine intervention as possible

Normal posture:
- operational overseer, not daily operator

Typical responsibilities:
- configure or enable providers
- review sync health and readiness issues
- retry failed imports or correct unusual timing/feed issues
- enter league context and use commissioner/member capabilities when
  exceptional intervention is required

## Commissioner

Goal:
- run an easy, fun league with minimal overhead

Normal posture:
- member-plus within a league, not a separate high-friction operator role

Typical responsibilities:
- create contests quickly from templates
- invite members and manage league participation
- use the same team and entry tools as members
- help teams within the league when administrative intervention is needed

## Member / Team Owner

Goal:
- participate in contests through a team, create entries, make selections, and
  follow leaderboard changes

Typical responsibilities:
- manage their team
- create and edit entries while allowed
- follow live scoring and standings

## System

Goal:
- automate the normal product loop

Typical responsibilities:
- import events and participants from providers
- resolve operational timing
- derive contest fields
- update scoring and leaderboards automatically
