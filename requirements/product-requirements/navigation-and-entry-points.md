# Navigation And Entry Points

## League Context

League context is the main organizational entry point for commissioners and
members. Contest creation starts here, and league membership/team operations
remain anchored here.

## Team Context

Entries are always team-scoped. Team pages should show the team's active and
historical contest participation, and entry creation/editing should preserve
clear team context.

## Contest Context

Contest pages should show contest status, rules summary, entries, and the
contest-specific field/selections experience. Contest pages are valid places to
launch team-scoped entry creation.

## Role Behavior

- root admin should not require a separate day-to-day navigation universe for
  routine product use
- commissioners should use the same team and contest surfaces as members for
  most ongoing play behavior
- specialized admin-only routes should stay focused on exceptional operational
  concerns such as provider or sync issues
