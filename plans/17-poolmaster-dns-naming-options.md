## 🛠 Strategy & Technical Recommendations

### 1. The "Hub and Spoke" Redirect Strategy
To make your brand easy to access on mobile while keeping a professional "long-form" identity, use a **301 Permanent Redirect**:
* **Primary Brand:** `ultimatepoolmaster.com` (Use this for SEO and marketing).
* **Short Link:** `upm.app` (Use this for quick typing and mobile shortcuts).
* **Setup:** Most registrars (Porkbun, Namecheap, Cloudflare) offer free URL forwarding. Point the short domain to the long one in your DNS settings.

### 2. Registry Notes for 2026
* **.app Requirement:** If you choose `upm.app`, it requires an **SSL certificate (HTTPS)** to load. Most modern registrars include this for free.
* **Privacy:** Ensure your registrar includes **WHOIS Privacy** for free to prevent your personal contact details from being public.
