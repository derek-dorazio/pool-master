# Deferred Contest Rules

Items removed from v1 scope. Can be added later when the core pool experience is solid.

## NFL Player-Based Scoring
- PPR, Half-PPR, Standard fantasy scoring
- 16+ stat categories (passing, rushing, receiving, kicking)
- Position-specific scoring
- **Reason**: Too many scoring categories to maintain data accuracy. Market already saturated by ESPN, Yahoo, DraftKings.
- **When to add**: After perfecting simpler sports (golf, soccer, hockey)

## Season-Long Fantasy with Weekly Management
- Weekly roster moves, trades, waiver wire
- Full-season standings across multiple weeks
- **Reason**: Weekly management is a different product category. Focus on single-event pools first.

## DFS (Daily Fantasy Sports)
- Daily salary cap lineups
- Contest lobbies with prize pools
- **Reason**: Regulated differently, requires real-money infrastructure, saturated market.

## Survivor Double Pick + Buybacks
- Double pick: must pick 2 winners per period
- Buybacks: re-entry after elimination
- **Reason**: Added complexity. Basic survivor (single pick, strikes) covers 95% of use cases.

## Advanced Soccer Stats
- Tackles, interceptions, saves per player
- Clean sheet tracking (GK/DEF position-specific)
- Key passes, shots on target
- Penalty saves
- **Reason**: Hard to source reliably. Goals + assists covers the core experience.

## Advanced NBA Stats
- Steals, blocks, turnovers
- Three-pointers made
- Double-double / triple-double bonuses
- **Reason**: Simplify to points/assists/rebounds for pool play.

## F1 Season-Long Fantasy
- Draft drivers for full season
- Budget changes per race
- **Reason**: Per-race pools are simpler and more engaging.

## NASCAR Season-Long Fantasy
- Full season draft
- **Reason**: Per-race pools are the primary use case.
