# Hero Image Prompt — 03 Sunday Prime

**Save output as:** `brand/concepts/03-sunday-prime/hero.jpg`
**Aspect ratio:** 4:5 portrait (e.g. 1200×1500 or 1024×1280)
**Target mood:** gameday, broadcast, prime-time energy — *"Sunday Night Football meets your group chat"*

---

## Prompt (paste into Midjourney / DALL-E 3 / Gemini / Nano Banana)

> A cinematic wide-angle photograph from inside a packed stadium at dusk, just after kickoff. Stadium floodlights blaze overhead, casting dramatic golden rim-light across the field. The stands are a blur of navy-blue seats and gold jerseys, out of focus. Light motion blur on a single player mid-stride in the foreground, slightly right of center. A slight fog of stadium breath hangs in the cold evening air. Shot on 35mm film, high contrast, desaturated navy shadows, warm gold highlights, faint red accent in stadium signage. Editorial sports photography, reminiscent of an SI Magazine cover or a primetime NFL broadcast intro. Generic team colors only — navy, gold, red. No recognizable team logos, no readable jersey numbers, no text. Leave the upper third relatively open for headline copy.

**Midjourney tail:** ` --ar 4:5 --style raw --stylize 400`
**DALL-E 3 note:** request "portrait orientation, cinematic sports photography."

---

## Negative prompt / avoid

- No NFL, NBA, MLB, NHL, NCAA, or any real-world team logos, wordmarks, or color schemes that match a specific franchise
- No readable jersey numbers, nameplates, or stadium signage
- No daytime lighting — this must read as evening/prime-time
- No empty stadium — we need packed-stands energy
- No single-sport specificity (should feel "American sports" broadly)
- Avoid digital/HUD overlays — the overlay happens in CSS

## Alt direction — abstract broadcast

> An abstract broadcast-graphic composition: bold diagonal stripes in navy and gold, over a soft-focus stadium-lights bokeh background, with a faint white grid suggesting a field. Film grain, cinematic, minimal, the aesthetic of a premium sports-network title card.

## How the hero slot uses this

The hero sits on the left ~620px-wide pane. Big italic headline *"Every pick. Every week. One place."* overlays the upper half. Keep the **upper third** with lower contrast so the headline pops. The dark navy overlay in CSS will deepen the shadows — aim for more highlight data than shadow data.
