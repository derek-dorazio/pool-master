# Hero Image Prompt — 02b Playbook (Light)

**Save output as:** `brand/concepts/02b-playbook-light/hero.jpg`
**Aspect ratio:** 4:5 portrait (e.g. 1200×1500 or 1024×1280)
**Target mood:** editorial, Swiss, architectural — *"Linear docs meets a Dieter Rams showroom, shot in daylight"*

**Palette:** warm off-white `#fafaf8` · ink black `#0a0a0a` · electric chartreuse-lime `#c8ff3d`. Nothing else.

---

## Prompt (paste into Midjourney / DALL-E 3 / Gemini / Nano Banana)

> A minimalist editorial 3D render on a warm off-white backdrop (#fafaf8). Floating geometric shapes arranged like a tournament bracket: matte black rectangular cards with crisp thin black outlines, arranged in a precise grid, connected by thin black lines. One single bright chartreuse-lime block (exact color #c8ff3d) sits prominently off-center at a slight angle, outlined in 1.5px pure black ink, casting an offset hard shadow in pure black — like a sticker on paper. A faint grid of thin light-grey lines fades into the background. Soft natural daylight from the upper-left casts crisp clean shadows. Generous negative space in the upper third. Inspired by Swiss graphic design, Linear's docs page, Vercel's brand art, and Dieter Rams-era product photography. High contrast, crisp edges, zero texture noise. No text, no logos, no people. Palette: warm off-white (#fafaf8), pure black ink (#0a0a0a), electric chartreuse-lime (#c8ff3d) — no other colors.

**Midjourney tail:** ` --ar 4:5 --style raw --stylize 200`
**DALL-E 3 note:** request "portrait orientation, minimal Swiss-design editorial render."

---

## Negative prompt / avoid

- No warm or dark tones other than the pure palette — no beige, cream, taupe, grey beyond light-grey grid lines, and absolutely no blue, red, orange, or pink
- No gradients, no glow, no soft light — this is crisp daylight with hard shadows
- No text, numbers, labels, or readable characters
- No skeuomorphic textures (no paper fibers, noise, grain, linen, wood, marble)
- No recognizable sports iconography
- No ray-traced glossy reflections
- Avoid "AI tech" visual clichés — circuit boards, robots, HUDs

## Alt direction — flat photographic

> A flat-lay photograph on a warm off-white paper surface. A single bright chartreuse-lime square of cardstock, outlined in thick black marker, sits at a slight angle with a hard offset black shadow drawn behind it. Around it: a few small black index cards arranged in a bracket layout, connected by thin black ink lines. Soft daylight, crisp shadows, high contrast. Editorial, minimal, Swiss-design aesthetic.

## How the hero slot uses this

The hero sits on the left ~620px-wide pane. Headline *"Run the league. Skip the spreadsheet."* renders black with the word "Skip" in a lime highlight block. Keep the **upper-left quadrant** mostly open so the headline overlay has breathing room. The lime block should sit in the **right half** of the composition. Unlike the dark variant, this direction relies on **crisp edges, hard shadows, and generous white space** — not atmospheric glow.
