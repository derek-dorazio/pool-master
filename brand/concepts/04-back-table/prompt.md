# Hero Image Prompt — 04 Back Table

**Save output as:** `brand/concepts/04-back-table/hero.jpg`
**Aspect ratio:** 4:5 portrait (e.g. 1200×1500 or 1024×1280)
**Target mood:** warm, handmade, indie community — *"our group chat, but with scoring"*

---

## Prompt (paste into Midjourney / DALL-E 3 / Gemini / Nano Banana)

> A warm hand-drawn flat-lay on textured cream-colored linen paper. Composition elements: a folded teal and terracotta felt pennant stitched by hand, a sketched playoff bracket in fountain-pen ink with names crossed out and re-written, a coffee-stained ring, a wooden dice, a small polaroid of a group of friends cheering (faces out-of-focus), a hand-lettered note reading "week 4" in casual cursive. Natural side-light from a kitchen window, soft shadows, visible paper fiber texture, subtle coffee stains. Illustrated in a mixed-media style — part photography, part collage, part ink drawing. Palette: warm cream paper, terracotta red-orange, deep teal, sepia ink. Feels like a scrapbook page. No modern tech, no screens, no logos of real teams.

**Midjourney tail:** ` --ar 4:5 --style raw --stylize 350`
**DALL-E 3 note:** request "portrait orientation, illustrated flat-lay collage style."

---

## Negative prompt / avoid

- No phones, laptops, tablets, TVs
- No real-world team logos or league names
- No cold colors or slick digital aesthetic
- No photorealistic human faces (keep any people soft-focus / anonymous)
- No generic "cozy" stock imagery clichés (candles, lattes with latte art, knit blankets)
- Avoid Instagram filter looks — aim for editorial and intentional

## Alt direction — just the pennant

> A single handmade felt pennant, half teal and half terracotta with hand-stitched letters "PM" in cream thread, hanging against a rough plaster wall. Warm natural light, shallow depth of field, slight paper grain. Editorial, minimal, artisanal.

## How the hero slot uses this

The hero sits on the left ~620px-wide pane. Headline *"Your league, your rules, your people."* in deep teal serif overlays the upper third. The concept's vibe benefits from imperfection — visible brush strokes, ink bleed, paper folds. The darker overlay in CSS is subtle here; the image itself should carry the warmth.
