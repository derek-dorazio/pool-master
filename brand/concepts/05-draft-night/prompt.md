# Hero Image Prompt — 05 Draft Night (Neon)

**Save output as:** `brand/concepts/05-draft-night/hero.jpg`
**Aspect ratio:** 4:5 portrait (e.g. 1200×1500 or 1024×1280)
**Target mood:** late-night draft-party energy — *"arcade sports, 1am, your league just kicked off"*

**Palette:** midnight navy `#0b1030` · electric cyan `#22e7ff` · electric lime `#a8ff3d` · deep indigo `#4361ee` · white

---

## Prompt (paste into Midjourney / DALL-E 3 / Gemini / Nano Banana)

> A moody cinematic photograph of a neon sign glowing against a midnight-blue brick wall at 1am. The sign reads "DRAFT NIGHT" in retro-cursive **electric cyan** neon tubes with an **electric-lime green** underline glow — the tubes are slightly buzzing, with the "D" flickering. A faint arcade light spills from an open doorway nearby, casting soft bokeh in cyan and lime. Rain-slick pavement in the foreground reflects the cyan and lime glow. Shot on 35mm film, Kodak Portra, shallow depth of field, grainy, slight chromatic aberration, deep shadows. Vaporwave meets Blade Runner meets a Brooklyn dive bar, but cooler and cleaner than hot-pink neon — more "arcade sportsbook" than "nightclub." No people in frame. Palette: midnight navy, electric cyan, lime-green glow, deep indigo shadows, crisp white highlights. No other readable text beyond "DRAFT NIGHT."

**Midjourney tail:** ` --ar 4:5 --style raw --stylize 500`
**DALL-E 3 note:** request "portrait orientation, cinematic neon photography."

---

## Negative prompt / avoid

- **No pink, magenta, hot red, or warm orange tones** — this is the whole point of the swap
- No people or identifiable faces
- No corporate sports logos (NFL/NBA/etc.)
- No text other than "DRAFT NIGHT" on the sign itself
- No daylight or flat lighting — must read as night
- Avoid CGI / game-engine look — should feel like actual film photography
- No excessive clutter; composition should leave the upper-left quadrant open for headline copy

## Alt direction — abstract neon

> An abstract composition: glowing neon tubes bent into an angular bracket shape against pure midnight navy, electric cyan and lime-green, soft halation, rain bokeh, film grain. No text, no figures. Editorial, graphic, dreamlike.

## How the hero slot uses this

The hero sits on the left ~620px-wide pane. Headline *"Draft day, every day."* renders white-to-cyan gradient over the upper third. Place the neon sign **right half or lower-right** so copy overlay has breathing room. The dark CSS overlay deepens shadows; aim for more midtones than deep blacks so the cyan/lime tubes read vividly.
